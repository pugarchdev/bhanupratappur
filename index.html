This page provides information about the official WhatsApp Helpdesk service of the East Bhanupratappur Forest Division. Citizens can use this service to report forest related incidents and seek assistance from the Forest Department.

About East Bhanupratappur Forest Division

The East Bhanupratappur Forest Division operates under the Forest Department of the Government of Chhattisgarh. The division is responsible for the protection and management of forest resources, wildlife conservation, and prevention of illegal forest activities in the Bhanupratappur region.

The division works towards sustainable forest management, biodiversity protection, and community engagement in forest conservation.

Official WhatsApp Citizen Helpdesk

The East Bhanupratappur Forest Division has introduced an official WhatsApp Helpdesk to allow citizens to easily report forest related issues and receive assistance from the Forest Department.

Through this WhatsApp service, citizens can submit information and complaints related to forest incidents directly to the concerned authorities. This digital service helps improve response time and enables better coordination between the public and forest officials.

Services Available Through WhatsApp

Citizens can use the WhatsApp Helpdesk to report the following:

  • Forest fire incidents
  • Wildlife related incidents
  • Illegal tree cutting
  • Forest land encroachment
  • Illegal transportation of forest produce
  • Other forest related complaints

Citizens may also upload photos, videos, or location details while reporting incidents to assist the Forest Department in taking prompt action.

Complaint Registration and Tracking

Once a complaint is submitted through the WhatsApp Helpdesk, the system generates a reference number for the complaint. Citizens can use this reference number to track the status of their complaint.

Forest officials review the complaints and take appropriate action based on the information received.
